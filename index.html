Quality Control
Do This. Never That.
✓ DO
✓Warm orange/amber light only — always
✓Keep the candle lit and IN the frame for lifestyle shots
✓Shoot every background at both flat lay AND 45° angle
✓Use Portrait Mode or shallow depth of field for close-ups
✓Clean all surfaces between shots — no fingerprints
✓Hands must have clean, dark-toned manicured nails
✓Minimum 40 raw images before you finish
✓Leave breathing space between props — do not overcrowd
✗ DON'T
✗NO flash, ring light, or bright front-facing light
✗NO cool white or daylight bulbs — warm only
✗NO bright/white backgrounds — no white marble
✗NO coloured props outside the brand palette
✗NO visible logos except Truwhispers packaging
✗NO overhead fluorescent or office-style lighting
✗NO chipped nails, rings, or busy jewellery in hand shots
✗NO photographer reflection visible in product surface
